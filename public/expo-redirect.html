Connect to Fixer App

Use Expo Go to connect to your Fixer app development environment.

Open in Expo Go

If the button doesn't work, try these methods:

Method 1: Direct Link

exp://YOUR_REPLIT_URL

Method 2: Development Client

exp+fixer://expo-development-client/?url=https://YOUR_REPLIT_URL

Method 3: Manual Entry

Open Expo Go and tap "Enter URL" then enter:

exp://YOUR_REPLIT_URL

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure Expo Go is installed on your device
  • Ensure your phone and computer are on the same network
  • On iOS, you may need to tap "Open in Expo Go" when prompted
  • If nothing works, try scanning the QR code from the terminal
